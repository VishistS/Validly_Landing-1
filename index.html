Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Validly

How does Validly validate business ideas?

Validly uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze market data, competitor information, and customer demand patterns. We provide comprehensive insights including market size, competition analysis, target audience identification, and risk assessment—all in minutes instead of months.

What types of businesses can use Validly?

Validly is designed for entrepreneurs, SaaS founders, enterprise business development teams, and anyone looking to validate a business idea. Whether you're building a startup or expanding an existing business, our platform provides the insights you need to make informed decisions.

How accurate are the validation results?

Our validation results are based on real-time market data and comprehensive analysis. While no tool can guarantee 100% accuracy, Validly provides data-driven insights that significantly reduce the risk of pursuing unviable ideas and help you focus on opportunities with the highest potential.

When will Validly be available?

We're currently in development and plan to launch soon. Join our waitlist to get early access and be among the first to experience how Validly can transform your business idea validation process.

What's included in the validation report?

Each validation report includes market analysis, competitor intelligence, target audience insights, MVP roadmap, risk assessment, and a comprehensive validation score. You'll receive actionable insights to guide your business decisions.

How is Validly different from any other GPT wrapper?

Validly is specifically designed for business idea validation, not general conversation. We use specialized prompts, market data integration, and structured analysis to provide actionable business insights rather than generic responses. In addition to this, we integrate with a suite of API tools to ensure that your business idea validation process is efficient and effective.
